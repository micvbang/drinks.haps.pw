## Ingredients
- 5 parts bourbon whiskey
- 1 part simply syrup
- 2 dashes of Angostura
- 1 lemon peel
- 3-4 ice cubes

## Notes
- Uncertain which whiskey is better for this drink

## Procedure
1. Mix all ingredients in wide glass
2. Stir
3. Twist lemon peel over the rim of the glass
4. Enjoy
