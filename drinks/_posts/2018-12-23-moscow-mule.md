## Ingredients
- 3 parts vodka
- 1 part lime juice
- 6 parts ginger beer
- 4 ice cubes
- mint for garnish


## Procedure
1. Pour ice cubes, lime juice, and vodka in serving glass
2. Add ginger beer
3. Stir
4. Add garnish
5. Enjoy
